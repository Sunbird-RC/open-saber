What is a Registry?

  1. The data is considered a representation of truth by using digital signatures to ensure only verifiable and trusted data is shared.Digitally signed data available via these APIs brings in tamper resistance, electronic verifiability, and portability of data.
  2. MSDE becomes a source of data (eg on training center locations, students, courses, etc.) via sharing data openly through APIs. This ensures machine readable access rather than just human access via portals or apps. Enabling automated access by external systems makes data use scalable and cost efficient for these third parties. The ease of use of APIs also prevents others from creating copies of the data and serving as a separate source.

Registries are thus a programmatic interface for verifiable data designed as a layer above internal existing MSDE databases, enabling other ecosystem players and developers (public, private, and non-profit) to build a variety of market solutions -- such as rating applications or tailored locator applications in different languagess -- for end users.
